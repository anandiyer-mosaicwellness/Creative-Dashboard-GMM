How to Refresh This Dashboard Weekly
  1. Download the weekly Asset Association Report from Google Ads (master account level)
  2. Rename the file: Asset association report (N).xlsx — where N is the next number
  3. Place it in the uploads folder alongside all existing files
  4. Open build_dashboard.py, scroll to the WEEK_GROUPS section, and add a new line:
    ("18 May 2026 - 24 May 2026", ["Asset association report (N).xlsx"])
  5. Run: python build_dashboard.py
  6. Open the new MM_YT_Dashboard.html — done!
💡 From next week onwards you'll download from the master account level which combines all accounts automatically — just one file per week needed.
